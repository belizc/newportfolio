Turtles

Akumal "The Place of Turtles"

AkumaI is a city in Mexico known for it’s beautiful and vibrant sea green sea turtle population. I have been lucky enough to visit twice to swim with these wonderful animals. While admiring them as they swam around me a local tour guide shared some interesting turtle facts. Inspired by this visit enjoy some of my favorite turtle facts on this page that I have collected over time.

  1. Turtles have an “Egg Tooth” which they use to crack their shells when hatching
  2. Sea turtle gender is determined by the temperature of the nest, a warmer nest means more females will be born and a colder one means more males
  3. Crush from "Finding Nemo" is a green sea turtle, they are known to occasionally dine on jellyfish but mostly subsist off of algae which is why they have a green hue
  4. A sea turtle's shell is called a carapace and it's actually part of their skeletal structure.
  5. Sea turtles cannot hide in their shells like other turtles, but luckily they have flippers that help them swim fast to escape predators
